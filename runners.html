Pacers

If you would like to use pacers, you are welcome to do so. However, they will be required to pay a prorated entry fee, based on the percentage of the course they run. They will accordingly be eligible only to that percentage of the winnings, given their runner is the winner. Unless their runner decides not to split the winnings with them.
